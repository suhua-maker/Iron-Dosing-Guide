IRON DOSING GUIDE
for anemia in pregnant women
Using target haemoglobin (Ganzoni formula) ¹ ² ³
Total dose (mg) = Body weight (kg) X [Target Hb - Actual Hb] (g/dl) X 10 X 0.24 + Iron Store
Please key in the patient's body weight and current hemoglobin level:
The body weight used is the pre-pregnancy weight or weight at booking visit.
Enter the patient's actual hemoglobin level (g/dl).
Target haemoglobin level for treatment: 13.0 g/dl
Iron body store: ⁴ If < 35kg: 15mg x weight (kg) If => 35kg: 500mg
Exact Iron Dose Calculated: 0 mg
The total dose can be rounded off to 100mg increments.
Method of administration
Iron Sucrose Similars (ISS) can be given either as slow IV injection (undiluted) or IV infusion which must only be diluted in sterile 0.9% sodium chloride solution (normal saline).
Intravenous infusion: Maximum single tolerated dose 7 mg/kg once a week (max 500 mg)
Test Dose (for new patient): 20mg in 20ml over 15 minutes
